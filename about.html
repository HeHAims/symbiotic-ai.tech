Our Vision

Symbiotic AI LLC is built on the idea that intelligence should be a cooperative force between humans and machines. We design cognitive systems that do more than generate answers — they think, reason, translate, reflect, and adapt to the user's emotional and cognitive state.

Founder

Symbiotic AI was founded by Hilarion Hernandez, a researcher focused on mathematical cognition, AI reasoning, and human-aligned machine intelligence. The goal is to create tools that help students, researchers, and engineers understand mathematics and symbolic systems at a deeper level.

What Makes Symbiotic AI Different?

Unlike traditional AI systems that rely purely on large language models, Symbiotic AI integrates:

• CMIS – mathematical reasoning
• ULMFI – formula linguistics and explanation
• SSHAICA – emotional reasoning layer
• AND_RES4 – symbolic-boolean fusion engine

These components create an AI that is not just “smart,” but cognitively structured.

Industry Alignment

Symbiotic AI is aligned with EdTech, STEM learning, AI-assisted tutoring, reasoning engines, and adaptive cognitive systems. We build tools that help people understand — not just compute.

NVIDIA Integration

Symbiotic AI uses NVIDIA GPUs for model training, LoRA optimization, symbolic-neural hybrid workflows, and large-scale reasoning simulations. These technologies allow us to scale to real-time cognitive AI.
